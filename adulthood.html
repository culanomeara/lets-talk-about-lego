Childhood

This is one of my first ever sets and definitely one of my all-time favourites. I remember playing with this and other space lego sets for hours. A bigger version of it appeared in the LEGO Movie and only recently has been rebooted with a brand new upgraded set. I found it again recently when clearing out my attic and it still gives me pleasure!

PRICE: It was released in 1979 at a price of IR£29. If you were clver enough to put it away(still in box), you'd be able to sell it for around €1500! Extremely rare to find a new, unopened one of these. To be fair, who buys LEGO and doesn't open it?
