Childhood

This is one of my first ever sets and definitely one of my all-time favourites. I remember playing with this and other space lego sets for hours. A bigger version of it appeared in the LEGO Movie and only recently has been rebooted with a brand new upgraded set. I found it again recently when clearing out my attic and it still gives me pleasure!

PRICE: It was released in 1979 at a price of IR£29. If you were clver enough to put it away(still in box), you'd be able to sell it for around €1500! Extremely rare to find a new, unopened one of these. To be fair, who buys LEGO and doesn't open it?

Another oldie but a goodie! This classic castle set has everything a child could want. A Castle, horses, knights, weapons and endless possibilities for fun. It could be combined with other castle sets to get the full castle battle experience.

PRICE: It was released in 1978 and cost approxiamtely IR£37. The latest sale for a used set was €500.

The Formula 1 Racing Car was an absolute classic set. For a boy, this completed the trio of must-haves: A Space set, a Castle set and now a Racing Car set. Lookign back now, it was. a basic design and build but it was so playable that it gave hours of fun.

PRICE: It was a relatively cheap set from memory, costing less than IR£5. You can get your hands on a bran dnew, unopened version fro €210!
